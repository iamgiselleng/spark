# AI Readiness Discovery

A short, self-scoring questionnaire for small & medium businesses to spot where AI can save them time and money. Based on the **SPARK** framework:

- **S** — Spot the leaks
- **P** — Pilot one small win
- **A** — Apply what works
- **R** — Review the results
- **K** — Keep building

## What it gives you

About 7 minutes, 18 questions. At the end you get:

1. An overall AI Readiness score (0–100%) with a band: *Just starting*, *Getting going*, *Building momentum*, or *Well underway*.
2. A breakdown across the five SPARK steps.
3. Your **Top 3 Pain Points (Leaks)** — the areas worth piloting first.
4. A suggested next step you can take this week.

No tech background needed. Built for SMB owners, internal teams, and advisors.

## How to use

- Share the live link with a colleague, prospect, or your team.
- Fill it in on phone or laptop — it's mobile friendly.
- At the end, **Print or save as PDF**, or **Copy summary to clipboard** to paste into an email or note.

Nothing is sent to a server. All scoring happens in the browser; refreshing the page clears it.
